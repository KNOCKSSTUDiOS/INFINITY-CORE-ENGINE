# INFINITY‑CORE‑ENGINE — BRAND LAW

The INFINITY‑CORE‑ENGINE brand is governed by strict, non‑negotiable laws.  
These laws protect identity, purity, consistency, and mythic presence across all platforms, visuals, sounds, and modules.

---

## Identity Pillars

The brand is built on three fused pillars:

- **Silent Power** — monolith presence  
- **Cosmic Energy** — living pulse  
- **Engineered Precision** — technical mastery  

Every asset must reflect all three.

---

## Symbol Law

The **Infinity‑Core Monolith** is the root symbol.

Rules:

- Always vertical  
- Always ascendant  
- Always heavy  
- Always cosmic  
- Always engineered  

Forbidden:

- rotation  
- distortion  
- recoloring outside palette  
- softening  
- cartoon styling  

The monolith is sacred.

---

## Wordmark Law

The official wordmark:

# **KNOCKSSTUDiOS™**

Rules:

- lowercase **i** is mandatory  
- heavy, industrial geometry  
- engineered spacing  
- Titan Alloy™ or Voidglass™ material  
- subtle Fluxline Blue™ glow  

Forbidden:

- alternate spellings  
- alternate cases  
- thin fonts  
- playful fonts  
- stretched or compressed versions  

The wordmark is identity.

---

## Material Law

Only six core materials exist:

- **Obsidian Core™** — void base  
- **Sentinel Steel™** — industrial weight  
- **Voidglass™** — holographic clarity  
- **Fluxline Blue™** — energy  
- **Astral Pulse Violet™** — cosmic charge  
- **Emberstone™** — heat‑forged power  

New materials must be forged, not improvised.
